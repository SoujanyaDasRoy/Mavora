Operating as a solo founder in 2026 is easier than ever, thanks to a robust ecosystem of micro-services and developer platforms.

## Hosting & Frontend

Platforms like Vercel and Netlify remain the standard for hosting static frontends. With instant deployments and global edge networks, you can ship frontends in seconds.

## Database & Authentication

Micro-authentication engines like Clerk and edge databases like Turso or Supabase allow you to manage persistent user data without maintaining dedicated infrastructure.
