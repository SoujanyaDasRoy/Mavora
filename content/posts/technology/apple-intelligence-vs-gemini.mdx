The smartphone wars of the 2010s were fought on camera specs and screen quality. The next decade will be decided on AI hardware. Apple and Google are both racing to put the most capable on-device model in your pocket — and the gap between them is narrower than you might think.

## What Apple Intelligence Actually Does Well

Apple's approach prioritizes privacy and integration. Tasks like summarizing your email, rewriting a message, or generating images never leave your device. The latency is near-instant because there's no network roundtrip involved.

## Where Gemini on Android Has the Edge

Google's on-device Gemini Nano handles conversational queries with more nuance. Its integration with Google Search gives it an information advantage that Apple's more walled garden approach struggles to match.

## The Real Winner: The User

Competition means both platforms are shipping features faster. What felt like magic a year ago is table stakes today.
