Before you dismiss this as a hobby article, consider that tactile feedback genuinely reduces typing errors — something a 2024 Stanford study on developer ergonomics confirmed. Your keyboard is the interface between your ideas and the code editor.

## The Switch That Matters Most

Linear switches (like Cherry MX Red) are fast and quiet, favored by gamers and those in open offices. Tactile switches (like Brown) offer feedback without the noise of a full clicky key. Clicky switches (Blue) sound satisfying but will earn enemies in any co-working space.

**Keychron K2 Pro**: The best value pick. Wireless, compact, hot-swappable. Under $100 and works beautifully with macOS and Windows.

**Nuphy Air60 v2**: Ultra-slim profile for developers who travel. Doesn't feel mechanical but gets close.
