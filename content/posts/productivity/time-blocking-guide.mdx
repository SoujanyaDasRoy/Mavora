Time blocking is one of the most effective scheduling systems for knowledge workers, developers, and designers.

## What is Time Blocking?

Instead of maintaining a generic to-do list, time blocking requires you to partition your day into distinct blocks of time, dedicated to specific tasks. This eliminates cognitive overload and decision fatigue.

## Designing Around Your Energy Cycle

Always schedule your most cognitively taxing tasks—like writing, designing, or coding—during your highest-energy blocks, which for most people is early in the morning.
